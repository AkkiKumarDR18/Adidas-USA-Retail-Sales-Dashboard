# 👟 Adidas-USA-Retail-Sales-Dashboard
An advanced Excel BI project analyzing $900M+ in sales data. Features automated Pivot Tables, dynamic YoY variance tracking, and interactive regional performance slicers.

### *Advanced Business Intelligence & Data Analytics with Excel*

## 🌟 Executive Summary
This project is a comprehensive **Business Intelligence (BI) Dashboard** developed to analyze Adidas' retail footprint across the United States. Utilizing a dataset of over 9,000 transactions, the dashboard transforms raw sales data into strategic insights regarding regional dominance, retailer efficiency, and product category growth.

## 📊 Key Analytical Insights
* **Revenue Overview:** Tracked a total sales volume of **$899M** with an operating profit of **$332M**.
* **Regional Performance:** Identified the **West Region** as the primary revenue driver ($269M+), followed by the Northeast ($186M).
* **Retailer Growth:** Performed **Year-Over-Year (YoY) Variance** analysis, highlighting Foot Locker and West Gear as key strategic partners with the highest sales volume.
* **Profitability Analysis:** Analyzed **Operating Margins** across sales methods, revealing that while In-store leads in volume, Online channels maintain competitive margins.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation
* **Dynamic Dashboarding:** Integrated Slicers for **Region, Retailer, and Timeline**, allowing stakeholders to filter data across 12+ months instantly.
* **Data Modeling:** Used **Advanced Pivot Tables** to aggregate multivariate data including Sales Method, Product Category, and Geography.
* **Automated Calculations:** Built custom formulas for **Operating Margin %** and **Sales Variance** to track business health.
* **Data Visualization:** Developed a unified UI featuring Trend lines, and Ranking bars for high-level executive reporting.
